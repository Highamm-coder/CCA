## 04 — Features

*Feature blocks. Lead with the user benefit, not the technical capability. Each block: headline + 2–3 sentences + optional supporting detail.*

---

### Feature 1 — Full Production Dashboard

**Label:** `PRODUCTION DASHBOARD`

**Headline:**
> Your service, sequenced and production-ready.

**Body:**
> Ordo is the complete picture of Sunday morning — order of service, wireless mic assignments, stage inputs, musician roles, and production cues, all organized in one clean, editable view that's live for every team member the moment you save.

**Supporting detail:**
> Auto-saves as you type. No export, no attachment, no "did you get the updated version?"

---

### Feature 2 — Wireless Microphone Assignment

**Label:** `WIRELESS MICROPHONES`

**Headline:**
> Every mic accounted for, before the doors open.

**Body:**
> Assign each wireless channel to a person and a role. Headsets, handhelds, lavalieres — tracked by name, not by memory. Know at a glance who's carrying what and where they're needed.

**Supporting detail:**
> Mic status visible to the whole team. No more "whose mic is that?"

---

### Feature 3 — Stage Inputs

**Label:** `STAGE INPUTS`

**Headline:**
> The board, mapped.

**Body:**
> Log every instrument, musician, and routing assignment for the service. Ordo's stage input view gives your engineer a clean, scannable reference — structured the way your board is actually set up, not the way a spreadsheet happens to be formatted.

**Supporting detail:**
> Location, instrument, musician, and routing — all in one row.

---

### Feature 4 — Planning Center Integration

**Label:** `PLANNING CENTER`

**Headline:**
> Your service plan, already in Ordo.

**Body:**
> If your team plans services in Planning Center, Ordo meets you there. Import your order of service, song list, and team assignments directly — so your production dashboard is built before anyone touches the board. No retyping. No reconciling two documents on Sunday morning.

**Supporting detail:**
> Planning Center handles the plan. Ordo handles the production. Together, they cover the whole service.

**Alternate headline:**
> Plan it in Planning Center. Run it in Ordo.

**Alternate body:**
> Ordo connects to Planning Center so your order of service, personnel, and schedule flow straight into your production dashboard. Your team spends less time building the sheet and more time preparing the service.

---

### Feature 5 — Liturgical Calendar

**Label:** `LITURGICAL CALENDAR`

**Headline:**
> Always in season.

**Body:**
> Ordo displays the current liturgical season so your team is oriented within the church calendar without having to look it up. Advent, Lent, Ordinary Time — the rhythm of the year, present in the dashboard.

**Supporting detail:**
> The ordo is the order of service. The season is its context. We keep both in view.

---

### Feature 6 — Production Notes

**Label:** `PRODUCTION NOTES`

**Headline:**
> Everything the team needs to know. Right there.

**Body:**
> Cues, reminders, last-minute changes — Ordo's production notes field is always visible and always editable. Auto-saves as you type. If it matters for Sunday morning, it goes in the notes.
