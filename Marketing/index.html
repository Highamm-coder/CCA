Introducing Ordo

One organized place for everything your team needs to know.

Ordo is the full production dashboard built around the specific rhythms of the liturgical service. The complete order of service, wireless mic assignments, stage inputs, production notes, and liturgical calendar — all in a single, immaculately organized interface.

Your service, sequenced and production-ready.

Ordo is the complete picture of Sunday morning — order of service, wireless mic assignments, stage inputs, musician roles, and production cues, all organized in one clean, editable view that's live for every team member the moment you save.

Wireless Microphones

Every mic accounted for.

Assign each wireless channel to a person and a role. Headsets, handhelds, lavalieres — tracked by name, not by memory.

Stage Inputs

The board, mapped.

Log every instrument, musician, and routing assignment for the service in a clean, scannable reference.

Planning Center

Your plan, imported.

Import your order of service and team assignments directly — dashboard built before you touch the board.

Liturgical Calendar

Always in season.

Ordo displays the current liturgical season so your team is oriented within the church calendar automatically.

Liturgical Intelligence

Built around the book you use.

Ordo doesn't just know the season—it knows the rubrics. Select your tradition during setup to automatically align your dashboard with the correct lectionary, proper texts, and service templates.

Structured by tradition.

Service templates know their own structure. A Sunday Eucharist maps directly to the run sheet with the correct liturgical sequence and naming.

The Collect of the Day.

The correct Collect flows directly from the lectionary to the dashboard. The production team can follow exactly what is being prayed, exactly when it happens.

Integrated lectionary.

The Old Testament, Psalm, Epistle, and Gospel display automatically for each service. The team knows the passage, the reader, and the required microphones.

Canticles and propers.

When the calendar dictates specific canticles or proper texts for Morning and Evening Prayer, Ordo pre-populates the correct music items.
